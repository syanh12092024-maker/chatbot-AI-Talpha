Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our automated messaging assistant ("AI Closer", "we", "us") handles information when you interact with our Facebook Pages through Facebook Messenger. By sending a message to one of our Pages, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information we collect

When you message our Pages on Messenger, we may receive and process:

  • Your Facebook-scoped user ID (PSID) and public profile info (name) provided by Messenger;
  • The content of messages you send to us (text, and images you choose to send);
  • Order-related details you voluntarily provide (name, phone number, delivery address, product choice).

2. How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide product information and customer support;
  • To process and arrange Cash-on-Delivery (COD) orders you request;
  • To generate helpful replies using an AI language model (see "Third parties" below);
  • To hand the conversation to a human staff member when needed.

3. Third parties

To generate conversational replies, message content may be processed by our AI provider, Anthropic (Claude API). We do not sell your personal information. We may share order details with our own fulfillment/logistics partners solely to deliver your order.

4. Data retention

We keep conversation data only as long as needed to provide support and fulfill orders, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

5. Your choices

You can stop interacting with the assistant at any time. To request access to, or deletion of, your data, contact us using the details below.

6. Children

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will reflect changes.

8. Contact

For any privacy questions or data requests, message the relevant Facebook Page directly, or email: hoangthiendetk1@gmail.com.
